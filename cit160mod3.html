Music Friends

My business idea would be an app that would connect people with similar taste and music and use their location to bring them together.

Primarily it would be used for finding others near you that would bring people together but it has other applications. It can be used to show bands where they are appreciated and liked in certain areas to help them with their tour locations.

Another application is to have events created by users to invite other users to join social events. It can include local musician performances or jam sessions between friends.

The application can be linked to Spotify or Pandora to better find the music taste from the user, or have it be put in manually.

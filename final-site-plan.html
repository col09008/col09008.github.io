Overview

Purpose

Our purpose is to help individuals with engineering ideas. The main focus would be to help them aquire patents for their ideas by the means of providing support, establishing network connections for the individauls, and helping them finding grants for projects and education.

Audience

The audience will be any type of engineering students into their Junior to Senior year of college that want to develope their idea into reality.

We have a list of professional connection to guide you through the transition from school to the workforce, and to give you tips and recommendations on employment and internships.
